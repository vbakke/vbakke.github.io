This page shows the vbakke/tryte-encrypt for encrypting an IOTA seed with a passphrase.

Paper wallets are not safe if anyone gets hold of the piece of paper, or gets a snapshot of your screen.

The vbakke/tryte-encrypt library is based on Bitcoin's BIP38 for this exact purpose, but modified to work with ternary seeds.
